Siddhartha Sahu

Hi! I am a PhD student in the David R. Cheriton School of Computer Science at the University of Waterloo.

I am researching graph processing systems with Prof. Semih Salihoglu as part of the Data Systems Group.

Research Interests

I want to build scalable and performant data management and processing systems. My current research is exploring new ideas for large-scale analytical processing of graph data.
